About me

I'm a final year mechanical engineering student at the University of Sydney with a focus in simulation-driven iterative design. I've also been lucky to be part of the sustainable aviation industry through Dovetail Electric Aviation, where I was a Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) engineer that helped validate existing CAD as well as participate in the drag-reduction optimization process.
